Pakistan on the Global Stage

Explore how Pakistan is performing in economics, politics, sports, and more.

Economic Outlook

Pakistan's economy faces challenges such as inflation and external debt. However, the country is pushing forward with digitalization, tech startups, and economic reforms backed by international lenders like the IMF.

Geopolitical Role

Pakistan plays a key role in South Asian geopolitics, balancing its relations with China, the United States, and neighboring countries. It is also a central player in initiatives like the China-Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC).

Sports Achievements

Cricket remains a major unifier in the country, with Pakistan producing world-class players. The national team continues to perform on the international stage, especially in cricket and hockey.

Technology & Innovation

Pakistan's tech scene is growing rapidly. From fintech startups to AI research, the country is beginning to show potential in the global digital economy.

Culture and Influence

Pakistan's music, film, fashion, and food are gaining popularity globally, especially through its diaspora. Platforms like Coke Studio have boosted the global appreciation of Pakistani culture.
